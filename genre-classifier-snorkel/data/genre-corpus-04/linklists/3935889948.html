Explaination of the Link Page Titles:

Reference:.....These pages have links to numerous sites, or contain exceptionally large amounts of information.
Tribal:.....These are sites which claim to be "official" or "unofficial" Tribal home pages
Smaller: ..... These sites have just a few links or are limited in information
Art:.....These sites have, or are about, artwork, music, and photographs
General:..... These sites cover subject matters which do not fit elsewhere
Personal: ..... These are the home pages of individuals
Rings: ..... These are Web Rings dedicated to North American Indians
Language: ..... These sites deal with Indian Languages
Media: ..... These sites are for publications, books, newsletters, e-mail lists, Radio and Television
Map: ..... These sites feature maps
Genealogy:..... These sites deal in genealogy, check here to research your family
History: .....These sites deal primarily in historical information
Powwow: ..... These sites cover Powwows and other special events
Misc.: ..... These sites are interesting or useful places which may not be directly related to Indians
Biography:..... These sites feature biographical information on Indians or people who made a major impact on Indians
Information:..... These sites contain information on Tribes, but do not claim to be a Tribal home page
Commercial:..... These sites all have something to sell
