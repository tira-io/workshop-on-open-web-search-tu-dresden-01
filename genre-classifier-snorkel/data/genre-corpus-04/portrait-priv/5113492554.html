Research:

BIOMAPS-Project (Project Coordination): Biodiversity Mapping for Protection and Sustainable Use of Natural Resources / World Map of Phytodiversity
  • Phytodiversity of the Americas in a global context: spatial patterns, scale dependence, qualitative aspects (My Ph.D.-Project) 
  • African Biodiversity:  Spatial patterns of vascular plant diversity in a changing environment (As part of the BIOTA Africa framework)
  • The relation of biodiversity and geodiversity
Ecuador (1994, 1997): In my graduate thesis I studied the tree species composition, structure and biogeography of two montane forests in western Ecuador (Reserva Florística-Ecológica Río Guajalito, Prov. Pichincha & Reserva Natural Integral Otonga, Prov. Cotopaxi (project page)). These studies form part of a joint project of the Institute of Botany, Bonn and the Pontifícia Universidad Católica del Ecuador, Quito (PUCE) investigating epiphyte diversity. 
PDF file of: Mutke 2001: Forest structure and tree species composition of the submontane rainforest at Río Guajalito. 
Venezuela (1993, 1997): As a part of the Inselberg-Project of the  Institute of Botany, Bonn I studied the vegetation of shallow depressions on rock outcrops (inselbergs) in southern Venezuela. This work was a continuation of a practical training in field botany I joined in 1993 under the supervision of A. Gröger* / Dr. W. Barthlott. *now at Botanic Garden Munich
Peru (1995): In 1995 I joined the BRYOSTRAT-Project (PD Dr. Kürschner, Berlin) in NE-Peru and studied some parameters of the forest along a transect from lowland forest to the Páramo.

Curriculum vitae

 
19. April 1971 born in Brühl, Germany
1990 Graduated Kopernikus-GymnasiumNiederkassel (Abitur 1990), Germany 
10/1990 - 07/1996 Studies of  Biology at the University of Bonn, Germany (major in Botany, minor subjects: Microbiology and Soil Science; Diploma Thesis about the Floristics, forest structure and Biogeography of a montane rain forest in western Ecuador)
1/1993-3/1993 Practical training in field botany in southern Venezuela (Vegetation of rock outcrops (inselbergs))  under the supervision of A. Gröger* / Dr. W. Barthlott.         *now at Botanic Garden Munich
9/1994-12/1994 Practical training in field botany in the "Reserva Florística-Ecológica Río Guajalito" (Pichincha / Ecuador) under the supervision of Lic. Jaime Jaramillo, Ponifícia Universidad Católica del Ecuador (PUCE)
4/1995-3/1996 Assistant at the Institute of Botany, University of Bonn
9/1995 BRYOSTRAT-Project (PD Dr. Kürschner, Berlin) in NE-Peru (studies of some structural parameters of the forest along a transect from lowland forest to the Páramo)
1/1997 - 6/2002 PhD student at the Institute of Botany, University of Bonn (Prof. Barthlott): Project Coordination of a Biodiversity Mapping Project with a personal focus on the Neotropics (BIOMAPS project)
1/1997 -  Member of the "Bonn Botanisch Führungsservice" in the Botanic Garden Bonn (guided tours in the garden)
1997 - 2001 Design of the Homepages of the Dept. of Systematics and Biodiversity, Institute of Botany, Bonn and of the Botanic Garden Bonn
4/1997-5/1997 Studies on the vegetation of rock outcrops in southern Venezuela as a part of the inselberg project of the Institute of Botany, University of Bonn
5/1997-7/1997 Studies on the floristics of the understory and the epiphytes of two montane rain forests in western Ecuador (Reserva Río Guajalito & Reserva Otonga) as a part of the epiphyte project of  the Institute of Botany, University of Bonn and the Ponifícia Universidad Católica del Ecuador (PUCE)
8/1997 - 7/1998 Scientific support for the Secretariat of the new German national initiative of DIVERSITAS (DIVERSITAS-Deutschland)
10/1998- Author for the "Lexikon der Biologie" (Spektrum Verlag): headword Biodiversity and others
2001-2003 Research Associate at the Institute of Botany, University of Bonn, Co-ordinator of the new BIOTA-BIOMAPS Project
10/2002 - permantent position at the Institute of Botany, University of Bonn
