Fried Green Tomatoes with White Cheddar Grits


INGREDIENTS:

Fried Green Tomatoes

1 cup panko bread crumbs
1/4 teaspoon cayenne pepper
1/2 teaspoon cumin
2 teaspoons chili powder
3 each green tomatoes
Flour (as needed)
1 cup buttermilk

White Cheddar Grits

1 cup vegetable stock
3 ounces Quaker quick grits
1/2 tablespoon roasted corn
1/4 pound white cheddar cheese, grated
Salt and white pepper to taste
1/8 cup heavy cream
3/8 tablespoon butter

Tomatillo Salsa

1/3 pound tomatillos, peeled
2/3 ounce garlic, whole and peeled
1/3 bunch cilantro (stems removed)
2/3 each jalapeno chile peppers, de-seeded
2 ounces Roma tomato
1/3 pound tomato
1/8 teaspoon kosher salt
1/8 teaspoon sugar

DIRECTIONS:

To prepare the tomatoes, slice tomatoes 1/2-inch thick. Dredge in flour and soak in buttermilk for five minutes. Set aside. Mix the dry ingredients together and roll the tomatoes in the mixture. Fry until golden brown in clarified butter. Serve with white cheddar grits and tomatillo salsa.

For the grits, heat the stock, then add remaining ingredients. Simmer to desired consistency (creamy).

To prepare the salsa, pull the skins off the tomatillos and simmer in water for 10 minutes until tender. Puree with remaining ingredients.


Recipe courtesy: Chef Michael Cross, Bacchanal Bistro and Wine Bar
